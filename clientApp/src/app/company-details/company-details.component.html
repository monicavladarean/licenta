Adventure Camps - about us

Our story

We started organizing summer camps for children 10 years ago, when we made our first project: a hiking camp for 6-12 year old children. We started as a team of 4 people that love kids, adventures and spending time in nature, our concern being that children don't spend as much time as needed outdoor, doing sports and socialising with other kids. So, we created Adventure Camps, a place where having fun while spending time outdoor is the main purpose. In each of our camps, children develop many abilities, from cooking or debating on a subject, to easily socialize and be independent.

Now, we are a big team of children loving people trained to make every kid's experience the best one possible, our staff participating every year at a one week training session with children psichologists, camp conseilours and planty of specialists in kids development, in order to assure the best quality for our camps!

Priorities and goals

For us, the most important things are children's safety and fun! Our motto is "healthy kids are happy kids", so our main priority is the wellbeing of all our participants, indifferent on the camp we are talking about.

Besides the phisical condition of the children, we are constantly working on their psihical development, moral rules being extremly important for us. In our camps, kids learn to communicate, state their opinions in any situation and be conscious that all the people arround them are important.

Fun is present in all the camps, being the purpose kids love Adventure Camps, based on their feedbacks! So, if you want to give your child an unique experience, where everything is created in order for him to have the best time of his summer, we're waiting for your registration!


Types of camps that we organize


Based on our experience and permanently listening to our partisipants and their wishes, we organize different categories of camps, having different themes and included activities. In this way, we ca satisfy every child and offer to each of our participants a memorable experience and a camp in which everyone can be with people that have the same interests, this also being the reason why Adventure Camps has staff members passionate about the themes that the camps have, in order to provide the best knowledge and energy possible.

We are permanently making researches to find out what kids really want from a summer camp, such that we can offer the the best holiday and give them the oportunity to practice the sports and activities that they love or want to learn.

At the moment, we are organizing the following themed summer camps, as follows: horse riding camps, swimming camps, hiking camps, biking camps, dance camps, cooking camps and language camps.
